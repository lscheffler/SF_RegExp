### To use the program:
* Pull SF RegExp
* Pull project
* Add *.\SF_RegExp\SF_RegExp.prg* to your project, make the files in *.\SF_RegExp* available to your project, in special
- .\SF_RegExp\wwDotNetBridge.\*
- .\SF_RegExp\SF_RegExp.dll
- .\SF_RegExp\SF_RegExp.pdb
